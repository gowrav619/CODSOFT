Magicfly Direct


At Magicfly, we endeavour to offer our customers the best. Magicfly Direct offers a set of exclusive benefits when you choose to book tickets directly on our website (www.magicfly.com)
or via the Magicfly mobile application (available on Apple iOS and Android). When booking direct gives you the best, why go anywhere else? Please note that Magicfly Direct benefits are
applicable for travel within India only (except when travelling on Economy Lite fares).





1. Free change/cancellation within 24 hours


Modify or cancel your tickets within 24 hours of booking at no extra
cost as long as your travel is within India and at least 7 days in future.





2. Earn 100 Bonus CV Points


Club Vistara members earn additional 100 CV Points every time they book directly with Vistara.





3. Special discounts


We offer exclusive discounts to friends, family, students, senior citizens and the Indian Armed Forces personnel.
Download the Vistara Mobile app or log onto www.airvistara.com to book tickets for
that well-earned break you’ve been putting off.
